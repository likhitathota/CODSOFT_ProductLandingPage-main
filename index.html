Coffee Benas

Coffee beans are the seeds of the Coffea plant, known for their rich aroma and complex flavors. They are primarily sourced from regions around the equator, such as Latin America, Africa, and Asia. The beans undergo a roasting process, which unlocks their distinctive taste profiles, ranging from fruity and floral to nutty and chocolaty.
